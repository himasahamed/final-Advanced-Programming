Help - Room Reservation System

  • Login: Use your username and password to access the system.
  • Add Reservation: Fill guest details, room type, and dates, then submit.
  • View Reservation: Enter Reservation No to see saved reservation details.
  • Generate Bill: Enter Reservation No to calculate total amount and print.

If you face issues, check your internet connection, restart the server, and ensure MySQL is running.
